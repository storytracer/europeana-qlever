


# Copyright management guidelines for cultural heritage institutions

![Page 1 Cover.jpg](../attachments/06620377-f1a2-4347-9cc6-2961a3fe6844.jpg)

The copyright management guidelines aim to support best practices in copyright management in cultural heritage organisations with the ultimate goal of developing a harmonised approach to copyright management. They are organised in five sections, and for each of them they provide tips on the workflow, who to involve, who to inform, what to document and how to start the discussion.

![](/wiki/s/-1239427862/6452/802adcc4887d24bbbc33b4b925aefd398e02f9ca/_/images/icons/emoticons/star_blue.png) *These guidelines were created by the* [*Europeana Copyright Community Steering Group*](https://pro.europeana.eu/page/europeana-copyright)*. They are licensed under the* [*CC BY 4.0 license*](https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by/4.0/)*. They were first published in March 2022. These guidelines do not constitute any legal advice.*

Adler, Justin Bonfiglio, Kristina Eden, and Brian S. Hall*](https://quod.lib.umich.edu/c/crmstoolkit/14616082.0001.001/1:7/--finding-the-public-domain-copyright-review-management-system?rgn=div1;view=toc)*.*

# Harmonise a specific approach to risk management for your organisation

Evaluate risk and risk appetite, and consider whether other tools are more appropriate to mitigating risk in addition to copyright

|  ⛳ **Workflow**                                                                     |  ![](/wiki/s/-1239427862/6452/802adcc4887d24bbbc33b4b925aefd398e02f9ca/_/images/icons/emoticons/star_blue.png) **Who to involve**    |  🗣️ **Who to inform**                                     |  📑 **What to document**                                      |  🖋️ **How to document**                                                  |  📲 **How to start the discussion**                                                                                                                       |
|:-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|:-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|:----------------------------------------------------------|:--------------------------------------------------------------|:-------------------------------------------------------------------------|:----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| Agree on approaches to copyright risk and risk appetite at the organisational level. | This includes the senior management, business managers, compliance teams, curatorial teams and digital staff.                        | All other teams across the organisation must be informed. | A risk framework or assessment template to inform compliance. | Internal policy documents, consider publishing online to benefit others. | Weigh and discuss the copyright risks and benefits. Show the risk of lack of copyright management. Show and compare the risks of open or closed licenses. |

# Phase 1. Foundation

Develop adequate workflows to deal with copyright at multiple stages of collection management across your organisation

## Acquisition

As far as possible, obtain copyright information, licences, and transfers at the time of acquiring collection materials

|  ⛳ **Workflow**                                                                                                                                                                                                                          |  ![](/wiki/s/-1239427862/6452/802adcc4887d24bbbc33b4b925aefd398e02f9ca/_/images/icons/emoticons/star_blue.png) **Who to involve**                    |  🗣️ **Who to inform**                                                                  |  📑 **What to document**                                                                                          |  🖋️ **How to document**                                                                                                                                                                            |  📲 **How to start the discussion**                                                                                      |
|:------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|:-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|:---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|:------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|:---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|:-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| <ul><li><p>Integrate copyright transfer or licence clauses into contracts</p></li><li><p>Prepare questionnaires on copyright ownership for donors and sellers</p></li><li><p>Create relevant metadata fields in inventories</p></li></ul> | <ul><li><p>Acquiring curator / purchaser</p></li><li><p>Organisational lead or team for acquisitions</p></li><li><p>Any legal advisors</p></li></ul> | <ul><li><p>Access</p></li><li><p>Communications</p></li><li><p>Marketing</p></li></ul> | Ownership of all forms of copyright in the collection materials, as far as known, and any known gaps in knowledge | <ul><li><p>Record copyright information within donation</p></li><li><p>Purchase agreements as far as possible</p></li><li><p>In collection metadata</p></li><li><p>Inventory records</p></li></ul> | Draft / discuss general donation agreements and purchase contracts that have a dedicated space for copyright information |

## Documentation and use of collection materials

Record copyright information about collection materials and incoming loans, keep records up to date, and ensure visibility of copyright information and licenses

|  ⛳ **Workflow**                                                                                                                                                                                                                                   |  ![](/wiki/s/-1239427862/6452/802adcc4887d24bbbc33b4b925aefd398e02f9ca/_/images/icons/emoticons/star_blue.png) **Who to involve**       |  🗣️ **Who to inform**                                                                                                                                     |  📑 **What to document**                                                                                                                                                                |  🖋️ **How to document**                                                 |  📲 **How to start the discussion**                                                                                                                                                                        |
|:---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|:----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|:----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|:----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|:------------------------------------------------------------------------|:-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| <ul><li><p>Maintain records - record changes to copyright status</p></li><li><p>Assess your records at a suitable level to the scale and complexity of the collection materials (eg. at item or collection level as the case may be)</p></li></ul> | <ul><li><p>Responsible curator</p></li><li><p>Documentation team</p></li><li><p>Licensing team</p></li><li><p>Access team</p></li></ul> | <ul><li><p>Communications</p></li><li><p>Senior management</p></li></ul> (inform about possible licensing / re-use possibilities according to assessment) | <ul><li><p>Known changes to copyright status (eg. death of author or acquisition of rights)</p></li><li><p>Known details of rights ownership, licences issued or received</p></li></ul> | Collection metadata/inventory. Use standardised licences where possible | <ul><li><p>React to circumstances, projects, changes to copyright status, etc.</p></li><li><p>Assess (parts of the) collection and possible licensing</p></li><li><p>Draft standard licences</p></li></ul> |

## Digitisation and reproduction

Ensure copyright in photographs and reproductions are cleared with those digitising the content

|  ⛳ **Workflow**                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  |  ![](/wiki/s/-1239427862/6452/802adcc4887d24bbbc33b4b925aefd398e02f9ca/_/images/icons/emoticons/star_blue.png) **Who to involve**    |  🗣️ **Who to inform**       |  📑 **What to document**                                                                                                                                                                            |  🖋️ **How to document**                                                         |  📲 **How to start the discussion**                                                                                                                                         |
|:----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|:-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|:----------------------------|:----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|:--------------------------------------------------------------------------------|:----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| <ul><li><p>Prioritise digitisation procedures where no new copyright emerges</p></li><li><p>Conclude contracts with third party digitisation providers or staff members which ensure copyright in digitisations (where arising) is the property of the organisation</p></li></ul> | <ul><li><p>Responsible curator</p></li><li><p>Digitisation manager or team</p></li><li><p>Any legal advisors</p></li></ul>           | Any relevant internal teams | <ul><li><p>Agreement over ownership of copyright in any contracts</p></li><li><p>Any licences received from third parties to enable digitisation or making available of digitisations</p></li></ul> | <ul><li><p>Collection metadata/inventory</p></li><li><p>Contracts</p></li></ul> | <ul><li><p>Check for best practices and examples within your jurisdiction and sector</p></li><li><p>Draft standard contracts for future digitisation projects</p></li></ul> |

## Digital access and re-use

Maintain clear policies on copyright and access to and re-use from your collection materials, and make guidance on copyright available or link to existing guidance.

|  ⛳ **Workflow**                                                                                                                                                                                                             |  ![](/wiki/s/-1239427862/6452/802adcc4887d24bbbc33b4b925aefd398e02f9ca/_/images/icons/emoticons/star_blue.png) **Who to involve**         |  🗣️ **Who to inform**                                                                                 |  📑 **What to document**                                                                                                                                                           |  🖋️ **How to document**                                             |  📲 **How to start the discussion**                                                                                                                                                                                                         |
|:-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|:------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|:------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|:-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|:--------------------------------------------------------------------|:--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| <ul><li><p>Define a top level access and re-use policy</p></li><li><p>Prepare and publish procedures for applying the policy</p></li><li><p>Prepare and publish guidance for users on understanding the policy</p></li></ul> | <ul><li><p>Access teams</p></li><li><p>Licensing teams</p></li><li><p>Digitisation manager</p></li><li><p>Business managers</p></li></ul> | <ul><li><p>Publication teams</p></li><li><p>Trading teams</p></li><li><p>Communications</p></li></ul> | <ul><li><p>All aspects of outward licensing from collections (eg. licences used, standard licence terms, prices, conditions, contact points)</p></li><li><p>Guidance</p></li></ul> | Published materials (eg website) and internal policies and guidance | <ul><li><p>Champion the value of licences and re-use statements for enabling use of the collections</p></li><li><p>Exemplify how missing licences might damage the collection (ie. limited legitimate reuse may be permitted)</p></li></ul> |

# Phase 2. Expansion

Nurture sufficient copyright knowledge and support across your organisation

## Supporting staff

Ensure staff in the organisation have enough training or support available

|  ⛳ **Workflow**                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    |  ![](/wiki/s/-1239427862/6452/802adcc4887d24bbbc33b4b925aefd398e02f9ca/_/images/icons/emoticons/star_blue.png) **Who to involve**    |  📑 **What to document**                                                                                                                                                                                                            |  🖋️ **How to document**                          |  📲 **How to start the discussion**                                                                                                                                                                 |
|:----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|:-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|:------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|:-------------------------------------------------|:----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| <ul><li><p>Establish copyright education levels for all roles</p></li><li><p>Design guidance and training packages targeted to each level</p></li><li><p>Work with human resources teams and managers to ensure that all staff receive guidance and training according to their level</p></li></ul> | <ul><li><p>Human resources teams</p></li><li><p>Managers</p></li><li><p>Compliance and copyright experts</p></li></ul>               | <ul><li><p>Copyright education levels</p></li><li><p>Recording the depth and breadth of information that must be known at that level</p></li><li><p>The amount of training required at that level (frequency, detail)</p></li></ul> | Internal records available to managers and teams | <ul><li><p>Show risks of lack of copyright knowledge as connected to the strategic objectives of the organisation</p></li><li><p>Check for external input</p></li><li><p>Benchmarking</p></li></ul> |

## Supporting administration

Senior management understands the importance of dealing with copyright adequately

|  ⛳ **Workflow**                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            |  ![](/wiki/s/-1239427862/6452/802adcc4887d24bbbc33b4b925aefd398e02f9ca/_/images/icons/emoticons/star_blue.png) **Who to involve**    |  📑 **What to document**                                                                                                                                                                            |  🖋️ **How to document**                          |  📲 **How to start the discussion**                                                                                   |
|:--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|:-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|:----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|:-------------------------------------------------|:----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| <ul><li><p>Specific copyright education level for senior management, which focuses on communicating the importance of copyright to different aspects of the organisation’s work and risks</p></li><li><p>Dedicated training/copyright induction processes for senior managers</p></li></ul> | <ul><li><p>Human resources teams</p></li><li><p>Senior managers</p></li><li><p>Compliance and copyright experts</p></li></ul>        | <ul><li><p>Copyright education level</p></li><li><p>Recording the level the information that must be known at this level</p></li><li><p>The amount of training required at this level</p></li></ul> | Internal records available to managers and teams | <ul><li><p>Case studies</p></li><li><p>Risks of not understanding copyright</p></li><li><p>Benchmarking</p></li></ul> |

# Phase 3. Integration

Integrate copyright needs in activities and projects across your organisation

## Short-term integration

The copyright requirements of every activity and project are assessed during the planning stage

|  ⛳ **Workflow**                                                                                              |  ![](/wiki/s/-1239427862/6452/802adcc4887d24bbbc33b4b925aefd398e02f9ca/_/images/icons/emoticons/star_blue.png) **Who to involve**    |  🗣️ **Who to inform**                                                                                    |  📑 **What to document**                                                                                                     |  🖋️ **How to document**    |  📲 **How to start the discussion**                                                     |
|:--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|:-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|:---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|:-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|:---------------------------|:----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| Ensure consideration of copyright is included on relevant plans and templates, eg. project proposal documents | Any team planning a project                                                                                                          | <ul><li><p>Senior managers</p></li><li><p>Copyright managers</p></li><li><p>Communications</p></li></ul> | <ul><li><p>The copyright role in the project</p></li><li><p>The copyright resource requirements of the project</p></li></ul> | Project plan               | Draft project proposal templates etc. with a dedicated space for copyright information. |

## Long-term integration

Adequate copyright resources are allocated to activities and projects

|  ⛳ **Workflow**                                                                                                                                                                                                       |  ![](/wiki/s/-1239427862/6452/802adcc4887d24bbbc33b4b925aefd398e02f9ca/_/images/icons/emoticons/star_blue.png) **Who to involve**    |  🗣️ **Who to inform**                                            |  📑 **What to document**                        |  🖋️ **How to document**    |  📲 **How to start the discussion**                            |
|:-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|:-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|:-----------------------------------------------------------------|:------------------------------------------------|:---------------------------|:---------------------------------------------------------------|
| <ul><li><p>Make the case for necessary copyright resources, based on the project plan (or similar)</p></li><li><p>Outline the risks of not having the resources and the benefits of having the resources</p></li></ul> | Project lead / steering group                                                                                                        | <ul><li><p>Senior managers</p></li><li><p>Managers</p></li></ul> | Risk assessment of lack of copyright management | Business case              | Show risks of lack of copyright management <br/>  <br/>  <br/> |
