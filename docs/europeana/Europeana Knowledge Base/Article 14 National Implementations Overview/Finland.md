| Comments / analysis                                         | <ul><li><p><strong>Object of protection: </strong>Photographs which are not a photographic work (lack of originality).</p></li><li><p><strong>Type of protection: </strong>Reproduction in original and modified form and making available to the public. Moral rights of attribution and integrity.</p></li><li><p><strong>Duration: </strong>50 since the picture was made.</p></li></ul> Unlike the Directive, the implemented law includes photographs only, not visual arts more widely. Also, according to implementation provisions the new rules are applied only to acts, contracts etc made after 3.4.2023.
