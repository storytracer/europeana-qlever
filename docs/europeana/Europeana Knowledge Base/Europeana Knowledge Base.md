Following up with the [Recommendations for how to improve the usefulness of guidance material](https://docs.google.com/document/d/1h_eKpFtowRGQ4NwvmIoI-jzNELyyTKOxrHBDJFaNdjc/edit) (December 2020), Europeana Foundation (EF) has updated the [Publishing guide](Publishing%20guide.md) , [EDM - Mapping guidelines](EDM%20-%20Mapping%20guidelines.md), [User Guides & Training](User%20Guides%20&%20Training.md) into web-based documentation using Atlassian’s Confluence as a knowledge base software. Alongside this, EF is actively and successfully using other Atlassian products, such as Jira and Trello, which greatly supports the work done internally at EF as well as the collaboration with the data partners.

In this section, we’ll show basic navigation hints that will help you get familiar with the knowledge base as our new space for documentation. For further information requests related to this space, please reach out to [adina.ciocoiu@europeana.eu](mailto:adina.ciocoiu@europeana.eu) or [henning.scholz@europeana.eu](mailto:henning.scholz@europeana.eu).

For those looking to have the information listed in the pages above in .pdf format, the Europeana Knowledge Base allows for exporting the content of a page or of the entire space in either a word/pdf format.

To export of a single page, follow the  **. . .**  symbol on the right-hand side of the page and chose between word or pdf:
